Frequently Asked Questions

When is the best time to get married in Goa? expand_more
The best season for a Goa wedding is between November and February when the weather is pleasant, dry, and cool. October and March are also good options for shoulder season rates.
How much does a destination wedding in Goa cost? expand_more
A typical 2-day wedding for 150 guests at a 5-star resort in Goa can range from ₹30 Lakhs to ₹60 Lakhs, depending on the venue, decor, and inclusions. Boutique options can start from ₹15 Lakhs.
Do you help with guest accommodation? expand_more
Yes! Most of our venue packages come with room block negotiation. We help secure the best room rates for your guests at the wedding venue or nearby hotels.
