Your Questions Answered

What is the ideal booking lead time for Umaid Bhawan? expand_more
Due to high demand and exclusivity, we recommend booking Umaid Bhawan Palace at least 12-18 months in advance, especially for dates between October and March.
Can we customize the catering menu? expand_more
Absolutely. The palace chefs are world-renowned and can craft bespoke menus ranging from authentic Rajasthani royal thalis to international gourmet experiences.
Is accommodation included for guests? expand_more
Wedding packages typically include a block of rooms and suites. Since the palace is also a functioning hotel, we can coordinate room allocation for your VIP guests ensuring a seamless stay.
