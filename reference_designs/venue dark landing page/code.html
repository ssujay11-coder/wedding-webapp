Frequently Asked Questions

What is the guest capacity for the Baradari Lawns?

expand_more

The Baradari Lawns can comfortably accommodate up to 750 guests for a reception or 500 for seated dining, offering a grand view of the palace facade.

Do you offer partial planning for Umaid Bhawan?

expand_more

Given the logistical complexity of this specific venue, we specialize in Full Service Planning to ensure the level of excellence required.

How far in advance should we book?

expand_more

Umaid Bhawan is one of the most sought-after venues globally. We recommend inquiring at least 12-18 months in advance to secure preferred dates.
