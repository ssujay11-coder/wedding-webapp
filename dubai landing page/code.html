Your Questions About Dubai Weddings, Answered

What is the best time of year to get married in Dubai?

expand_more
The ideal season for outdoor weddings in Dubai is from October to April, when temperatures are pleasant and range from 20°C to 30°C (68°F - 86°F). Indoor weddings are spectacular year-round thanks to world-class air-conditioned venues.

Do you handle legal paperwork for international couples?

expand_more
Yes, we have a dedicated legal team that assists with all necessary documentation and procedures for civil and religious ceremonies in Dubai, ensuring a seamless legal process for couples from all over the world.

Can you organize multi-day wedding celebrations?

expand_more
Absolutely. We specialize in comprehensive multi-day itineraries, including welcome parties, desert safaris, rehearsal dinners, the main ceremony, and post-wedding brunches.
