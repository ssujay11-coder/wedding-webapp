Common Questions

What is the best time for a wedding in Goa?

expand_more

The ideal season is between November and February when the weather is pleasant and cool. March to May is warmer but great for evening sundowners. The monsoon season (June to September) offers lush greenery for indoor weddings.

Do venues provide alcohol licenses?

expand_more

Most 4 and 5-star hotels have their own liquor licenses. For private villas or beach shacks, a temporary event license is required, which usually takes 2-3 weeks to process. Our team assists with all permit requirements.

Can we bring our own catering?

expand_more

Resorts typically require you to use their in-house catering, though some may allow outside caterers for specific specialty cuisines (like Jain food) with a "corkage" fee. Private villas often allow full flexibility with outside catering.
