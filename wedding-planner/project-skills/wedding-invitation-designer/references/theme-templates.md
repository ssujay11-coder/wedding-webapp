# Wedding Theme Design Templates

## Modern Minimalist

**Color Palette**: Black & white, grey scale, or monochrome with single accent
**Typography**: Clean sans-serif (Helvetica Neue, Montserrat, Futura)
**Layout**: Generous whitespace, asymmetric, geometric shapes
**Elements**: Simple lines, minimal ornamentation, bold typography
**Mood**: Sophisticated, contemporary, refined

## Classic Elegance

**Color Palette**: Ivory, champagne, gold, navy, burgundy
**Typography**: Traditional serif (Garamond, Baskerville, Didot) + script
**Layout**: Centered, symmetrical, formal
**Elements**: Borders, flourishes, monograms, classic ornaments
**Mood**: Timeless, formal, traditional

## Boho Chic

**Color Palette**: Terracotta, sage green, dusty rose, cream, ochre
**Typography**: Hand-lettered script, organic sans-serif
**Layout**: Asymmetric, flowing, natural
**Elements**: Watercolor, feathers, dreamcatchers, botanicals, macrame patterns
**Mood**: Free-spirited, artistic, natural

## Rustic Charm

**Color Palette**: Wood tones, kraft, forest green, burgundy, cream
**Typography**: Handwritten, vintage serif, slab serif
**Layout**: Informal, layered, textured
**Elements**: Wood grain, burlap texture, twine, wildflowers, mason jars
**Mood**: Warm, cozy, countryside

## Luxury Glamour

**Color Palette**: Black, white, gold, rose gold, champagne, blush
**Typography**: Art deco fonts, elegant scripts, high-contrast serif
**Layout**: Bold, dramatic, layered, geometric
**Elements**: Metallic foils, marble textures, geometric patterns, crystals
**Mood**: Opulent, sophisticated, show-stopping

## Garden Romantic

**Color Palette**: Blush pink, sage green, lavender, cream, soft peach
**Typography**: Flowing scripts, delicate serif, whimsical
**Layout**: Organic, flowing, botanical framing
**Elements**: Watercolor florals, vines, butterflies, garden illustrations
**Mood**: Soft, romantic, enchanting

## Beach Destination

**Color Palette**: Turquoise, coral, sandy beige, white, seafoam
**Typography**: Breezy scripts, casual sans-serif, nautical
**Layout**: Relaxed, beachy, organic
**Elements**: Seashells, waves, palm leaves, starfish, watercolor ocean
**Mood**: Relaxed, tropical, vacation

## Vintage Romance

**Color Palette**: Antique ivory, dusty pink, muted gold, sage
**Typography**: Vintage typewriter, old-world script, Victorian ornamental
**Layout**: Aged, ornate, layered
**Elements**: Lace patterns, vintage stamps, antique keys, old postcards
**Mood**: Nostalgic, sentimental, heirloom

## Art Deco

**Color Palette**: Black, gold, emerald, navy, champagne
**Typography**: Geometric deco fonts, bold sans-serif, glamorous script
**Layout**: Geometric, symmetrical, bold lines
**Elements**: Gatsby-style ornaments, sunburst patterns, geometric borders
**Mood**: 1920s glamour, bold, sophisticated
