# Vendor Management System

Professional vendor organization and tracking for seamless wedding planning.

## Essential Wedding Vendors

### Core Vendors (Must-Have)
1. **Venue** - Ceremony and/or reception location
2. **Catering** - Food and beverage service
3. **Photography** - Professional photos
4. **Officiant** - Ceremony leader

### Major Vendors (Highly Recommended)
5. **Videography** - Wedding film
6. **Music** - DJ or live band
7. **Florist** - Bouquets, centerpieces, ceremony flowers
8. **Wedding Planner/Coordinator** - Day-of coordination

### Additional Vendors
9. **Hair & Makeup** - Bridal beauty
10. **Cake/Dessert** - Wedding cake or dessert bar
11. **Invitations** - Stationery suite
12. **Transportation** - Guest shuttles, couple's car
13. **Lighting** - Uplighting, string lights
14. **Rentals** - Tables, chairs, linens, tents
15. **Photo Booth** - Guest entertainment
16. **Favors** - Guest gifts

## Vendor Information Tracking

For each vendor, track:

### Contact Details
- Company name
- Contact person name
- Phone number (primary, secondary)
- Email address
- Website
- Physical address
- Social media handles

### Service Details
- Service type/category
- Package selected
- Service date and time
- Setup time
- Breakdown time
- Location serving

### Financial Information
- Total cost
- Deposit amount
- Deposit due date
- Deposit paid (date)
- Balance due date
- Balance paid (date)
- Payment method
- Gratuity amount
- Cancellation policy
- Refund terms

### Contract Information
- Contract signed (date)
- Contract file location
- Key contract terms
- Cancellation deadline
- Force majeure clause
- Liability insurance details

### Communication Log
- Date of communication
- Method (email, call, meeting)
- Summary of discussion
- Action items
- Next follow-up date

## Vendor Checklist by Category

### Photographer Questions
- [ ] How many hours of coverage?
- [ ] How many photographers?
- [ ] Engagement session included?
- [ ] Digital files included? How many?
- [ ] Print rights?
- [ ] Turnaround time?
- [ ] Album included?
- [ ] Second shooter included?

### Caterer Questions
- [ ] Price per person?
- [ ] Service style (buffet, plated, family)?
- [ ] Tasting included?
- [ ] Bar service included?
- [ ] Alcohol provided or BYOB?
- [ ] Cake cutting fee?
- [ ] Staffing included?
- [ ] Rentals included (plates, glassware)?

- [ ] Ceremony and reception capacity?
- [ ] Available hours/time limits?
- [ ] Preferred vendor list or any required?
- [ ] Catering restrictions?
- [ ] Tables, chairs, linens included?
- [ ] Setup/cleanup included?
- [ ] Parking availability?
- [ ] Backup plan for weather?

### DJ/Band Questions
- [ ] Hours of performance?
- [ ] Ceremony music included?
- [ ] Cocktail hour music?
- [ ] MC services?
- [ ] Equipment provided (speakers, mics)?
- [ ] Lighting included?
- [ ] Song requests policy?
- [ ] Backup plan if DJ/band can't make it?

### Florist Questions
- [ ] What's included in package?
- [ ] Delivery and setup included?
- [ ] Rental items (vases, stands)?
- [ ] Ceremony flowers?
- [ ] Bouquets and boutonnieres?
- [ ] Centerpiece design?
- [ ] Teardown/removal service?

## Vendor Contract Review Checklist

Before signing any contract, verify:
- [ ] Names and date spelled correctly
- [ ] Exact services described in detail
- [ ] Total cost and payment schedule clear
- [ ] Cancellation and refund policy
- [ ] Weather/contingency plans (outdoor events)
- [ ] Liability insurance confirmation
- [ ] Overtime fees specified
- [ ] Delivery and setup times
- [ ] What's NOT included clearly stated
- [ ] Substitution policy (if vendor can't fulfill)
- [ ] Force majeure clause reviewed

## Vendor Tip Guidelines

Standard gratuity amounts:

- **Venue coordinator**: $100-300
- **Catering staff**: 15-20% (if not included)
- **Bartenders**: 10-15% or $50-100 each
- **Photographer**: $50-200 (optional, if exceptional)
- **Videographer**: $50-200 (optional)
- **DJ/Band**: $50-150 per person
- **Hair/Makeup**: 15-25%
- **Florist**: $50-100 (optional)
- **Transportation**: 15-20%
- **Officiant**: $50-500 (or donation to their organization)
- **Day-of coordinator**: $100-300

Tips typically given day-of in sealed envelopes with vendor name.

## Vendor Management Workflow

1. **Research Phase**
   - Create wish list of vendors
   - Read reviews and view portfolios
   - Check availability for wedding date

2. **Inquiry Phase**
   - Send inquiry emails
   - Schedule consultations/meetings
   - Request pricing and packages

3. **Comparison Phase**
   - Fill out comparison worksheets
   - Check references
   - Review contracts carefully

4. **Booking Phase**
   - Negotiate if applicable
   - Sign contracts
   - Pay deposits
   - Confirm in writing

5. **Management Phase**
   - Track payments
   - Schedule check-ins
   - Share timeline updates
   - Provide final details

6. **Final Confirmation**
   - Confirm all details 1-2 weeks before
   - Create vendor contact sheet
   - Prepare day-of payments/tips
   - Brief wedding party/coordinator
