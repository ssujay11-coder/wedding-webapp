# Client Wedding Manager

Professional wedding planner's client management system for tracking multiple weddings from booking to send-off.

## Service Package Types

### Full-Service Planning
**Duration**: 12+ months
**Scope**: Complete wedding planning from start to finish

**Includes**:
- Initial consultation and vision session
- Budget development and tracking
- Venue research and booking
- Vendor sourcing, negotiation, and management
- Design and styling direction
- Timeline creation and management
- Guest list and RSVP management
- Month-by-month planning guidance
- Unlimited communication
- Rehearsal coordination
- Day-of coordination and execution

### Partial Planning
**Duration**: 3-6 months before wedding
**Scope**: Mid-planning support and vendor completion

**Includes**:
- Initial consultation to assess current status
- Completion of vendor bookings
- Timeline and checklist creation
- Design refinement
- Monthly check-ins
- Contract review
- Rehearsal coordination
- Day-of coordination

### Day-Of Coordination
**Duration**: 4-6 weeks before wedding
**Scope**: Final details and wedding day execution

**Includes**:
- Initial meeting 4-6 weeks before
- Vendor confirmation and timeline distribution
- Ceremony rehearsal coordination
- Wedding day management (10-12 hours)
- Vendor setup supervision
- Timeline execution
- Problem-solving and troubleshooting
- Bridal party coordination

## Rehearsal Coordination

### Rehearsal Preparation
- [ ] Schedule rehearsal (usually night before)
- [ ] Create rehearsal timeline
- [ ] Print ceremony outline
- [ ] Prepare processional order
- [ ] Prepare recessional order

### Rehearsal Attendees
- Couple
- Wedding party (bridesmaids, groomsmen)
- Parents (both sides)
- Officiant
- Ceremony musicians (optional)
- Readers

2. Walk through processional (15 min)
3. Practice ceremony program (15 min)
4. Walk through recessional (10 min)

## Emergency Preparedness

### Emergency Kit Contents
- Sewing kit (needles, thread, safety pins)
- Stain remover pen
- Clear nail polish (stocking runs)
- Bobby pins and hair ties
- Band-aids and pain relievers
- Mints and gum
- Tissues and blotting papers
- Double-sided tape and glue dots
- Scissors and Swiss Army knife
- Phone chargers and backup battery
- Copies of timeline and vendor contacts
- Umbrellas (rain backup)

### Common Emergency Scenarios

**Vendor No-Show**:
- Activate backup vendor immediately
- Contact original vendor for status
- Document for potential legal/refund action

**Weather Issues**:
- Execute weather backup plan
- Communicate changes to all guests
- Update timeline as needed
- Stay calm and flexible

**Family Drama**:
- Separate conflicting parties
- Assign trusted person to manage
- Keep couple shielded from stress
- Handle discreetly and professionally

**Wardrobe Malfunction**:
- Use emergency kit
- Call on seamstress if serious
- Stay calm and solve quickly

**Timeline Running Behind**:
- Identify what can be cut or shortened
- Communicate with vendors
- Adjust timeline on the fly
- Prioritize key moments
