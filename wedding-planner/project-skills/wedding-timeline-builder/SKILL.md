# Wedding Timeline Builder

Create detailed, professional wedding timelines that ensure every moment of the wedding day runs smoothly.

## Timeline Types

### 1. Master Planning Timeline (12+ months out)
Monthly checklist from engagement to wedding day covering:
- Venue booking, vendor selection, dress shopping
- Invitations, RSVPs, guest management
- Final details, rehearsal, day-of coordination

### 2. Day-of Timeline (Wedding Day Schedule)
Hour-by-hour schedule for the wedding day:
- Getting ready, first look, photos
- Ceremony, cocktail hour, reception
- Key moments (first dance, toasts, cake cutting)
- Vendor arrivals and departures

### 3. Vendor Timeline
Detailed schedule for all vendors:
- Setup and breakdown times
- Delivery windows
- Performance/service periods
- Contact information and notes

### 4. Ceremony Timeline
Minute-by-minute ceremony flow:
- Processional order
- Readings, vows, rituals
- Recessional order
- Transition to reception

### 5. Reception Timeline
Detailed reception schedule:
- Grand entrance
- Dinner service
- Speeches and toasts
- Dancing segments
- Send-off

## Standard Wedding Day Timeline Template

```
2:00 PM - Hair & makeup begins (Bride & bridesmaids)

3:30 PM - Bride gets into dress
4:00 PM - First look (optional)
4:30 PM - Wedding party photos
5:00 PM - Guests begin arriving
5:30 PM - Ceremony begins
6:00 PM - Ceremony ends, cocktail hour begins
6:15 PM - Family photos
7:00 PM - Cocktail hour ends, reception doors open
7:15 PM - Grand entrance
7:20 PM - First dance
7:25 PM - Welcome speech
7:30 PM - Dinner service begins
8:15 PM - Toasts
8:45 PM - Cake cutting
9:00 PM - Parent dances
9:15 PM - Dance floor opens
10:45 PM - Last dance
11:00 PM - Grand exit/send-off
```

## Planning Checklist Milestones

### 12+ Months Before
- Set budget and guest count
- Choose wedding date
- Book ceremony and reception venues
- Hire wedding planner (if using)

- Book major vendors (photographer, caterer, DJ/band, florist)

- Start dress shopping
- Create wedding website

- Send save-the-dates
- Book hotel room blocks

- Book hair & makeup artists
- Plan honeymoon

### 3-5 Months Before
- Order invitations
- Plan ceremony details
- Book transportation
- Register for gifts
- Schedule dress fittings

### 2 Months Before
- Mail invitations
- Finalize menu and bar
- Confirm vendor details
- Apply for marriage license
- Final dress fitting

### 1 Month Before
- Create seating chart
- Finalize timeline with vendors
- Confirm final headcount

### 1-2 Weeks Before
- Final vendor confirmations
- Prepare vendor payments/tips
- Pack for honeymoon

### Day Before
- Rehearsal and rehearsal dinner
- Prepare emergency kit
- Relax and rest

## Timeline Building Workflow

1. **Gather Information**
   - Wedding date and start time
   - Venue(s) and locations
   - Vendor list and services
   - Special traditions or events
   - Number of guests
   - Any time constraints

2. **Determine Ceremony Time**
   - Work backwards from desired ceremony time
   - Factor in travel time between locations
   - Consider golden hour for photos
   - Account for seasonal daylight

3. **Calculate Key Blocks**
   - Getting ready: 3-4 hours
   - Photos: 1.5-2 hours
   - Ceremony: 30-45 minutes
   - Cocktail hour: 1 hour
   - Reception: 4-5 hours

4. **Add Buffer Time**
   - 15-30 min buffers between major segments
   - Account for unexpected delays
   - Build in flexibility

5. **Coordinate Vendors**
   - Add vendor setup/breakdown times
   - Include delivery windows
   - Note vendor meal times
   - Add contact information

6. **Review and Refine**
   - Check for conflicts or overlaps
   - Ensure realistic time allocations
   - Get feedback from planner/coordinator
   - Share with vendors for approval

## Time Allocation Guidelines

**Photography**
- Getting ready: 1-2 hours
- First look: 30 minutes
- Wedding party: 45 minutes
- Family formals: 30-45 minutes
- Couple portraits: 30-45 minutes
- Reception details: 30 minutes

**Ceremony**
- Processional: 5-10 minutes
- Ceremony program: 20-30 minutes
- Recessional: 2-3 minutes

**Reception**
- Grand entrance: 5 minutes
- First dance: 4 minutes
- Toasts: 15-20 minutes (3-4 speakers)
- Dinner: 45-60 minutes
- Cake cutting: 15 minutes
- Special dances: 10 minutes (2 songs)

## Customization Options

- **Cultural ceremonies**: Extra time for traditions
- **Multiple locations**: Travel time between venues
- **Outdoor weddings**: Backup plan timeline
- **Destination weddings**: Multi-day event schedule
- **Intimate weddings**: Condensed timeline
- **All-day celebrations**: Extended timeline with breaks
