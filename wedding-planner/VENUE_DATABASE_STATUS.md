## Content Requirements Per Venue

Each venue requires:
- ✅ Complete structured data (coordinates, pricing, capacity)
- ✅ 500+ word SEO-optimized long description
- ✅ 150-character short description
- ✅ 3-5 wedding spaces with capacities
- ✅ 10-15 key features
- ✅ 8-10 cuisine types
- ✅ SEO metadata (title, description, 10+ keywords)
- ✅ Contact information
- ✅ Awards and ratings
- ✅ 5-8 "Why Choose" points
- ✅ 3-5 "Ideal For" profiles
- ✅ Weather and season information
- ✅ Airport logistics
